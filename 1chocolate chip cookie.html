Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies with milk and dark chocolate chunks and savoury inclusions

total prep time

10 min.

serving

12 cookies

Tags : Desserts

instructions

step 1

Whisk together the sugar, brown sugar, melted butter, the egg, egg yolk, until fully emulsified. Finally, whisk in the salt, baking soda, and vanilla extract.

step 2

Add the flour 1 half at a time and fold with a spatula until combined. Do not overmix. Fold in the potato chips, milk chocolate, and dark chocolate. Cover and refrigerate for atleast 3 hours, ideally overnight.

Cooking Style 1

Small-sized cookies: 20-24 cookies, 180°C for 8-9 min

Cooking Style 2

Medium-sized cookies: 15-18 cookies, 180°C for 10-11 min

Cooking Style 3

Large-sized cookies: 8-9 cookies, 180°C for 14-15 min

step 3

Season to taste with flaky salt and let sit on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack

ingredients

100g granulated sugar

150g light brown sugar

113g unsalted butter, melted

1 large egg

1 large egg yolk

1 tsp salt

tsp baking soda

2 tsp vanilla extract

206g plain flour

85g milk chocolate

100g 60-70% dark chocolate

15g kettle cooked salted potato chips, crushed

tools

A bowl

A spatula

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Jug

Sieve
