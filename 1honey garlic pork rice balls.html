Honey Garlic Pork Rice Balls

Rice balls with a sweet garlicy pork filling

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 hours.

serving

1 serving

Tags : Sides

instructions

step 1

Cut broccoli into florets and place in a bowl

step 2

Drizzle oil over florets and add the spices, cheese and sugar

step 3

Mix thoroughly

step 4

Place in airfryer and cook at 200 for 12 minutes

ingredients

1 head of broccoli

½ tsp general seasoning

½ tsp taco seasoning

1 tsp garlic powder

1½ tsp brown sugar

10g parmesan

salt and black pepper

vegetable oil

tools

1 bowl

A wooden spoon

Measuring Spoons

Cheese grater
