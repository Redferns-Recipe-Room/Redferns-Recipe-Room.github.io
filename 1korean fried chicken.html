Korean Fried Chicken

Fried chicken covered in a korean gochujang sauce

prep time

2h 15 min.

cook time

30 hours.

serving

2 serving

Tags : Mains

instructions

step 1

Wash the rice and place in a rice cooker

step 2

Once rice has cooked, mix the warm rice with rice vinergar, sugar, salt and mirin

step 3

Cool in the fridge for 2 hours

step 4

In a bowl, place the drained tuna, adding kewpie mayo, mirin, sesame oil, salt and black pepper all to taste

step 4

On the sushi mat, place the nori rough side up, spoon rice onto half of it and squish flat and even. Place fillings such as tuna in a line

step 4

Wet the edge of the nori so it sticks and roll the sushi
