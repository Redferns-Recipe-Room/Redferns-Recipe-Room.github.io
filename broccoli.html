Fried Broccoli

Air fried broccoli with mixed seasonings

prep time

40 min.

cook time

2 hours.

serving

24 servings

Tags : Sides

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 150C. Grease and line cake pan.

step 2

Combine butter, chocolate and water in a large bowl. Microwave on 1000W for 3 mins, stirring every minute with a metal spoon, should be smooth.

step 3

Add and then mix caster sugar and vanilla to the mixture then egg then Sift in flours.

step 4

Pour mixture into prepared pan. Bake for 1:50 - 2 hours inserted skewer should have moist crumbs clinging.

For White Chocolate Ganache

step 5

Combine chocolate and cream in a bowl. Microwave, on 500W for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring every minute with a metal spoon, should be smooth.

step 6

Refrigerate for 1 hour, should be a spreadable consistency.

step 7

After spreading ganache on cake, set aside for 24 hours to set. To serve, cut cake with a warm knife to prevent ganache from cracking.

ingredients

400g butter chopped

500g white chocolate chopped

400ml milk

2 cups caster sugar

2 tsp vanilla extract

5 eggs, lightly beaten

2 cups plain flour

2 cups self-raising flour

For White Chocolate Ganache

600g White Chocolate, chopped

0.75 cup thickened cream

tools

2 Microwavable bowls

2 Mixing spoons
