Fried Rice

Basic fried rice with egg and veggies. add Chicken, Pork, Beef, Vegetable or Kimchi if you want.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

5 servings

Tags : Sides

instructions

Step 1

Melt 0.5 tablespoon of butter in a pan on 75% heat. Add egg, and cook until scrambled, stirring occasionally. Remove egg, and transfer to a separate plate.

step 2

Add another 1 tablespoon of butter to the pan and heat until melted.

step 3

Add carrots, onion, peas, salt and pepper. Sauté for about 5 minutes or until the onion and carrots are soft.

step 4

Increase heat to 100%, add remaining 1.5 tablespoons of butter, and stir until melted.

step 5

Add the rice, green onions, soy sauce and oyster sauce, stir until combined.

step 6

Continue sautéing for an additional 3 minutes to fry the rice while stirring occasionally.

step 7

Then add in the eggs, garlic and filling of choice and stir to combine.

step 8

Remove from heat, and stir in the 0.5 teaspoons of sesame oil and season with extra soy sauce, if needed.

ingredients

3 tbsp butter

2 eggs, whisked

2 carrots, peeled and diced

1 white onion, diced

0.5 cup frozen peas

3 cloves garlic, minced

salt and black pepper

4 cups of day old rice

3 green onions, thinly sliced

3 tbsp soy sauce

2 tsp oyster sauce

0.5 tsp sesame oil

tools
